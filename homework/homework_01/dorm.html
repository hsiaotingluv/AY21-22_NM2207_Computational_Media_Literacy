🛏 Single Room

Students have a choice of living in single rooms along the corridor or in a 6-bedroom suite.

🎤 Theme Rooms

A quintessential part of residential living involves sharing common spaces and facilities. These facilities aim to create a friendly supportive environment, cultivating spirited communities through social, educational and cultural interaction. CAPT has communal kitchens, laundry facilities and lounges for the comfort and convenience of residents. The College is also equipped with a reading room, seminar rooms, a multipurpose hall, dining hall and a common lounge (The Flying Seed) and theme rooms (The Workshop, The Arena and The Black Box).
